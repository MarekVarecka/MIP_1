\begin{abstract}
This paper talks about fake information and its influence on society. We discuss into its origins and how it can trick and deliberately mislead people. We also talk about how can we spot fake news and curb its spreading. It’s crucial to possess media (especially social media) literacy and be critical thinkers to prevent falling victim to misinformation. We will propose the use of AI as a tool to aid in the detection of fake news, which is a necessary skill in today’s information-saturated world. Individuals often struggle to keep up with on daily bases. Making this skill progressively vital.
\end{abstract}
